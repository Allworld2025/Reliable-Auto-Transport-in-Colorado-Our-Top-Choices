Preparing Your Vehicle for Transport

To ensure a smooth shipping process, follow these preparation steps recommended by industry experts:

  1. Clean Your Vehicle: Wash your car to easily identify pre-existing damage.
  2. Remove Personal Items: Clear out belongings, as carriers typically don’t cover items inside.
  3. Document Condition: Take photos and note damage during the pre-transport inspection.
  4. Check Fluids and Tires: Ensure proper tire pressure and minimal fuel (about ¼ tank).
  5. Disable Alarms: Turn off alarms to avoid disruptions during transport.
